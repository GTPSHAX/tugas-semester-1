About Us

  • MindBox

    MindBox, founded in 2024 by Rafie Hasannudin as Web developer, Ragil Prakoso and Revan Alexsia as UI/UX Designer, Gizila Akhmal and Brian Aldo as Documenter. This is a music streaming platform offering millions of songs with the best audio quality, no delays, and interruptions. With a user-friendly interface, personalized recommendations, and curated playlists, MindBox lets users discover, enjoy, and share music from artists worldwide. Experience seamless, high-quality music anytime, anywhere.

  • MindBox is committed to providing users with a diverse catalog, ranging from trending hits to undiscovered gems, all while ensuring exceptional audio quality. With features like curated playlists, offline listening, and a social sharing system, MindBox is poised to revolutionize the way people experience and share music globally. Whether you're at home or on the go, MindBox is your ultimate companion for music discovery and enjoyment.
